## Engineering Growth Journey

Over the past 10 years, I have grown from a backend developer focused on feature implementation into an engineer capable of designing distributed systems and thinking from both technical and business perspectives.

My experience includes enterprise systems involving:

- logistics platforms  
- IoT and real-time monitoring systems  
- event-driven architecture  
- scalable backend services  
- compliance-sensitive operational systems  

Through these projects, I gradually shifted my focus from coding individual modules to understanding how large-scale systems support operational efficiency, business workflows, and organizational scalability.

## Why MS in Information Systems

As my engineering experience expanded, I became increasingly interested in the intersection between technology, systems, and business strategy.

Pursuing an MS in Information Systems will help me strengthen my understanding of:

- enterprise architecture  
- business analysis  
- information systems strategy  
- data-driven decision systems  

My goal is to bridge engineering implementation with business-oriented system design, and contribute to building scalable, intelligent enterprise systems in the future.
